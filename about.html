About Me

Hey. My name is Eric, and I'm a sophomore at NJIT. My major is Business Information Systems (BIS) but I've always had a dream to start a clothing business one day.

I love fashion. It's one of the first things I do in the morning, and I love every second of it. Clothing is a form of art, and a very expressive way someone can express themselves. I grew up in the bustling streets of New Jersey so "street life" was always something I was accostomed too. Streetwear (what people usually wear in my neighborhood) definitely played a factor in my life. I was always in love with the expressive graphic designs companies like Supreme or Bape had and that's when I dreamt of making my own.

Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
